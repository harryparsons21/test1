About the Club

Tottenham Hotspur Football Club, commonly referred to simply as Tottenham or Spurs, is an English football club located in Tottenham, Haringey, London, that competes in the Premier League.
The club's home for the 2017–18 season is Wembley Stadium, as their former home of White Hart Lane has been demolished to make way for a completely new stadium on the same site.
Their newly developed training ground is in Bulls Cross on the northern borders of the London Borough of Enfield.
  • Nicknames: Spurs, The Lilywhites
  • Founded: 5th September 1882
  • Ground: Webley Stadium (as of 2017–18)
  • Capacity: 90,000
  • Chairman: Daniel Levy
  • Manager: Mauricio Pochettino
